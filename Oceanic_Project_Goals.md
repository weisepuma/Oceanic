# Oceanic Project Goals

1. Ensure the app does not harm living marine creatures.
2. Generate revenue streams that directly support whale conservation funds.
3. Educate users about whale migration, ocean ecology, and environmental challenges.
4. Provide an engaging and interactive experience that encourages users to participate in conservation.
5. Promote global awareness of marine life protection and sustainable ocean practices.
6. Encourage user loyalty and repeated engagement through seasonal campaigns and adoption programs.
