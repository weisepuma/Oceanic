Oceanic is an interactive app where you can adopt whale migration routes or the whale. Follow them across the ocean, learn about their journeys, and support whale conservation by adopting a route or the whale.

## What You Can Do

* Explore real whale migration routes.
* Adopt a route or the whale and support conservation funds.
* Learn about ocean ecology and marine life.
* Participate in seasonal campaigns and events.

## Why Oceanic?

* Fun and interactive way to connect with nature.
* Help protect whales and marine ecosystems.
* Educational content verified by science.
* Engaging experience with updates and new routes every season.

## Get Started

1. Sign up and choose a migration route or the whale.
2. Explore the route on interactive maps.
3. Support conservation by adopting a route or the whale.
4. Track your impact and enjoy learning!

Join us and be part of protecting the ocean and its amazing creatures by adopting a route or the whale!
